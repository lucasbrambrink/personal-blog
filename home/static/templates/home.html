Compressing A List

Suppose we want to compress a dictation of symbols (or a string) to show simply the number of successive repetitions.

'aaabbd' becomes 'a 3 b 2 d 1'

'1 1 1 3 3 3 4 4 4 4 4' becomes '1 3 3 3 4 5'

It would be fun to do this recursively. Let's go through the array, enumerating our aggregator with each step, and stop when the symbol changes. We can return, in a tuple, the rest of the array, the final count of our aggregator variable, and the symbol we were inspecting.
